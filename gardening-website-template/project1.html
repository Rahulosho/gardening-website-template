A Blend of Art and Nature

Project Details

Project Date: May 15, 2023 - August 30, 2023

Client: Andrew Whitman

Location: Surrey, England

Project Overview

Our team was commissioned to transform a 2-acre plot into a stunning formal garden that seamlessly blends traditional English garden design with modern artistic elements. The result is a harmonious space that invites exploration and contemplation.

Key Features

  • Topiary Sculptures: Custom-designed animal-shaped topiaries add whimsy and structure to the lawn areas.
  • Gravel Pathways: Carefully laid out to create a sense of journey and divide garden rooms.
  • Water Feature: A classic circular fountain serves as a focal point and adds soothing ambiance.
  • Mixed Planting Beds: Combining shrubs, perennials, and ornamental grasses for year-round interest.
  • Modern Sculptures: Strategically placed art pieces create visual intrigue and contrast with the natural elements.
  • Seating Areas: Multiple benches offer spots for rest and contemplation throughout the garden.

Technical Specifications

  • Irrigation System: Automated drip irrigation with moisture sensors
  • Lighting: Low-voltage LED landscape lighting for pathways and feature illumination
  • Soil Amendment: Custom-blended topsoil and compost mixture for optimal plant health
  • Hardscaping Materials:
    • Pathways: Crushed granite gravel (3/8" size)
    • Edging: Cor-Ten steel (1/4" thickness)
  • Plant Selection: Focus on drought-tolerant and native species to minimize water usage

Challenges and Solutions

The sloping terrain presented drainage issues, which we addressed by implementing a series of subtle terraces and installing a French drain system along the lower portions of the property. This ensures proper water management without disrupting the garden's visual flow.

Client Testimonial

"The team's attention to detail and ability to blend classic and contemporary elements has exceeded our expectations. Our new garden is not just a beautiful outdoor space, but a living work of art." - Andrew Whitman

Maintenance Plan

We've developed a comprehensive year-round maintenance schedule to ensure the garden remains in peak condition, including monthly visits for pruning, fertilization, and seasonal plantings.
